## 1. Pricing Strategy

### Tier Overview

| Tier | Price | Target User | Key Features |
|------|-------|-------------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Individuals exploring | Limited PRD generation, basic diagrams |
| Pro | $19/mo | Individual PMs/Devs | Full SDLC + basic design features |
| Design | $39/mo | Designers | Full design generation + Figma integration |
| Team | $49/mo | Small teams | Collaboration + full feature set |
| Enterprise | $99/mo | Large organizations | Custom integrations + advanced features |
| Agency | $149/mo | Agencies | White-label + client management |

## 2. Revenue Streams

### Primary: SaaS Subscriptions
- Target: 80% of revenue
- Focus on Team and Enterprise tiers for higher ARPU

### Secondary: API Usage
- Per-generation pricing for high-volume integrations
- Metered billing for code generation and design exports

### Tertiary: Professional Services
- Custom template creation
- Enterprise integration consulting
- Training and onboarding

## 3. Market Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market
- PM Tools: ~$2B
- DevTools: ~$20B
- Design Tools: ~$13B
- **Combined opportunity:** ~$35B

### Target Market Share
- Year 1: 0.01% (~$3.5M potential)
- Year 3: 0.1% (~$35M potential)

## 4. Growth Strategy

### Year 1: Foundation
- Focus on PM + Developer adoption
- Free tier for viral growth
- Pro tier for monetization

### Year 2: Expansion
- Enterprise sales motion
- Agency partnerships
- Third-party integrations marketplace

### Year 3: Scale
- International expansion
- Industry-specific solutions
- Platform ecosystem

## 6. Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Target: < $500 for Pro, < $2,000 for Enterprise

### Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Pro: $1,200 (12-month avg retention)
- Enterprise: $12,000 (24-month avg retention)

### LTV:CAC Ratio
- Target: > 3:1
